Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is one of my top artists, and she has been since I was just 12. She is such an icon, not only right now, but in the future as well. Each era has those legendary pop culture figures, like Marilyn Monroe or Elvis, and I truly believe Ariana Grande is going to be one of them. Her voice is just so incredible. She has crazy range, and can go so high I would have to scream to reach the notes she can hit. The control she has when she sings is also just amazing. As a singer, and as a person, I respect Ariana Grande a lot.
